**Pricing rationale (per Pricing Gap Closure Spec B1-5):**
- Clinic Monthly: $251.10/month = $279 * (1 - 0.10) — 10% clinic discount
- Clinic Annual: $2,863/year = $279 * 12 * (1 - 0.15) — 15% ceiling (10% clinic + 5% annual)
- Effective monthly on annual: $238.58/month
