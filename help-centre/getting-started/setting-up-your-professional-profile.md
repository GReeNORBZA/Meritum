Your professional profile is the first thing you complete when you sign in to Meritum for the first time. It covers Steps 1 and 2 of the onboarding wizard and cannot be skipped. The platform uses this information on every claim you submit, so it needs to be accurate.

## Step 1: Professional identity

1. Enter your **billing number**. This is the 5-digit Alberta Health Care Insurance Plan (AHCIP) practitioner ID that identifies you to Alberta Health. Every claim Meritum submits on your behalf carries this number.
2. Enter your **College of Physicians and Surgeons of Alberta (CPSA) registration number**. Meritum validates the format during onboarding. Your CPSA number ties your Meritum account to your professional registration and is included in the Information Manager Agreement (IMA) generated later in the wizard.
3. Enter your **legal first name** and **last name**. A middle name field is available but optional. Your legal name is used to pre-fill the AHC11236 Submitter Authorization form and the IMA, so it must match what Alberta Health and the CPSA have on file.

Both your billing number and CPSA registration number must be unique across the platform. If either is already associated with another account, the wizard will ask you to verify the information or contact support.

## Step 2: Specialty and physician type

1. Select your **specialty** from the dropdown list. The list is populated from the AHCIP specialty code table. You can type to search; start with your specialty name (for example, "Family" or "Cardiology") and the list will filter. Your specialty determines which Schedule of Medical Benefits (SOMB) health service codes appear first in searches, which governing rules the validation engine applies to your claims, and which modifier suggestions the advice engine offers.
2. Choose your **physician type**: General Practitioner (GP), Specialist, or Locum. This selection shapes your default workflow. Locum physicians see multi-location management tools from the start, while GPs and specialists work with a single primary practice location by default.

After you complete both steps, click **Continue** to move to business arrangement setup. You can return to edit your professional profile from your account settings at any time.

For the next step in onboarding, see [Adding your business arrangement numbers](/help-centre/getting-started/adding-your-business-arrangement-numbers).
