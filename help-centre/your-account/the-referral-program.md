Meritum's referral program rewards physicians who refer colleagues to the platform. When a physician you refer completes onboarding and pays their first month, you receive a $50.00 credit applied to your next invoice. The program is available to any physician with an active Meritum subscription.

## How referrals work

Every active subscriber has a unique referral code, visible in the referral section of your account settings. When a colleague registers for Meritum, they enter your referral code during the registration process. That links their account to yours as the referring physician.

The referral credit is triggered once both conditions are met:

1. The referred physician completes the full onboarding process (professional profile, business arrangement numbers, and at least one practice location).
2. The referred physician's first monthly payment is successfully processed by Stripe.

Once both conditions are satisfied, a $50.00 credit is added to your account. You do not need to take any action after sharing your code; the credit is applied automatically.

## Where credits are applied

Referral credits appear as a balance adjustment on your next Stripe invoice. When your subscription renews, Stripe subtracts the credit balance from the total charge before billing your card. If your credit balance covers the full invoice amount, no charge is made that cycle and any remaining balance carries forward.

For example, if you are on the standard monthly plan at $279.00 plus Goods and Services Tax (GST) and have a $50.00 referral credit, your next invoice would be $242.45 ($279.00 minus $50.00, plus 5% GST on the net amount).

Referral credits are not redeemable for cash. They can only be applied against Meritum subscription charges. Credits do not expire as long as your account remains active.

## Eligibility

Any physician with an active, paid Meritum subscription can participate in the referral program. This includes physicians on the standard monthly plan, the standard annual plan, and the early bird monthly plan.

The referred physician must be a new Meritum user. Self-referrals and duplicate referrals to an existing subscriber do not qualify. Each referred physician can only be linked to one referring physician.

## Limits

A single physician can earn a maximum of 10 referral credits per calendar year, which amounts to a $500.00 annual cap. This limit resets on January 1 each year. If you reach the cap, any additional referrals during that calendar year will not generate credits, though the referred physicians can still use your code to register.

## Referral credits and subscription tiers

Referral credits work the same way regardless of which subscription tier you or the referred physician are on. The credit is always $50.00 per qualifying referral, and it is always applied as a Stripe balance adjustment against your next invoice. Physicians on annual plans receive credits in the same manner; the balance carries forward and reduces the next renewal charge.

If you switch between monthly and annual billing, your accumulated referral credit balance transfers with you. Credits are tied to your Stripe customer account, not to a specific plan. See [Understanding your subscription](/help-centre/your-account/understanding-your-subscription) for details on the available subscription tiers and how billing works.

## Current availability

The referral program launches after Meritum reaches product-market fit. The referral code field exists in the registration flow but is currently disabled. Once the program goes live, all active subscribers will receive their unique referral code and can begin referring colleagues immediately. No action is needed on your part before launch; your code will be generated automatically when the feature is activated.

You can check the referral section of your account settings at any time to see whether the program is active and, once it is, to find your referral code and track your credited referrals.
