You can switch between monthly and annual billing at any time from your account settings. The change takes effect immediately, and any balance from your current billing period is prorated automatically.

## How to switch your billing frequency

1. Log in to Meritum and open **Account Settings** from the navigation menu.
2. In the **Billing** section, click **Manage Billing**. This opens the Stripe Customer Portal, where Meritum's payment processing is handled.
3. In the Customer Portal, select **Update plan** or **Change plan** (the exact label depends on Stripe's current interface).
4. Choose either **Standard Monthly** ($279.00/month) or **Standard Annual** ($2,790.00/year).
5. Confirm the change. Stripe shows you the prorated amount before you confirm, so there are no surprises.
6. Close the portal tab. Meritum receives the update automatically within a few seconds via a webhook from Stripe.

You do not need to contact support or wait for a manual review. The switch is self-serve and takes effect right away.

## What happens to your current balance

Stripe handles proration automatically. When you switch mid-cycle, Stripe calculates the unused portion of your current plan and applies it as a credit toward the new plan.

For example, if you are two weeks into a monthly billing cycle and switch to annual, Stripe credits the remaining two weeks of unused monthly charges and applies that credit to the annual charge. The net amount is charged to your card immediately.

If you switch from annual to monthly, the unused portion of your annual payment is applied as a credit to future monthly charges until the credit is used up.

## Annual savings

Annual billing costs $2,790.00 per year, which works out to $232.50 per month. That is approximately 17% less than the standard monthly rate of $279.00. Over 12 months, annual billing saves you $558.00 before tax.

Both plans include exactly the same features. The only difference is price and billing frequency. See [Understanding your subscription](/help-centre/your-account/understanding-your-subscription) for a full breakdown of what is included.

## Early bird pricing and annual billing

If you are on the early bird rate ($199.00/month), that pricing is available only as a monthly plan for your first 12 months. You cannot switch to annual billing while the early bird rate is active without losing that promotional rate.

Once your 12-month early bird period ends, your subscription moves to the standard monthly rate. At that point, you can switch to annual billing using the steps above. If you plan to continue with Meritum after the early bird period, switching to annual at that time locks in the lower per-month cost going forward.

## When the new billing cycle starts

Your new billing cycle starts on the day you make the switch. If you switch to annual on March 10, your annual renewal date becomes March 10 of the following year. If you switch to monthly, your next monthly charge is one month from the switch date.

Stripe sends a receipt to your account email address for every charge, including the prorated charge at the time of the switch.
